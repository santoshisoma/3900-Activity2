Lord shiva

Lord shiva and parvathi is a famous indian god. Shiva performs several roles as a Hindu deity. He is the great ascetic, the master of fertility, the master of poison. His combined roles are exemplary of a tendency in Hinduism to see complementary qualities in a single ambiguous figure .
